"Game Portal" is a simple web-app designed for Musubi that aggregates all the user data across the three gaming networks (Xbox, Playstation, and Steam), and allows a user to "Check-in" or tell their friends what they are playing, at the click of a button. There is a one time set-up where the user provides his/her usernames for each network, but the application remembers the usernames thereafter to provide the most convenience for the user. "Game Portal" aims to bridge the gap between the networks by allowing visibility of a friend's online/offline state between the networks simply through a check-in button. For example, if I were to be playing on Steam and a friend was playing a multiplayer game on Xbox, I would never be able to find out what they were playing without having to manually start up my Xbox and check their game status. With "Game Portal," all I would have to access is the one stop shop, where it tells you what games your friends are playing simply by checking the Musubi Feed. The seamless integration between the networks, and the simplicity of the application on the front-end, is exactly the aspect in which "Game Portal" prides itself.
